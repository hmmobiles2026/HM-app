1 Getting Started

Open the App URL above in any browser on your phone or computer — no installation needed.

  1. Go to your App URL
  2. Enter your Username and Password from the box above
  3. Press Sign In
Change your password after your first login. Go to Settings → Password, enter the password given above, then set a new one you will remember.
If you forget your password, contact support — details at the end of this guide.

3 Managing Stock

Go to Stock from the menu to manage your inventory.

Adding a product

  1. Click Add Product
  2. Fill in: Brand, Model, Part name, Cost price, Selling price, Stock quantity
  3. Set a Low stock threshold — you will get an alert when stock hits this number
  4. Click Save

Editing a product

Click the pencil icon next to any product to update its price, stock level, or any other detail.

Adding stock (Stock In)

Click the + icon next to any product, or go to Stock In at the top to add stock to multiple products at once. The owner receives an automatic Telegram notification when stock is added.

Deleting a product

Click the trash icon next to any product. You will be asked to confirm. Deleted products appear in a Recently Deleted section at the bottom of the stock list for 3 days — you can recover them if needed.

Low Stock Threshold — when stock falls to or below this number, the item appears as an alert on the dashboard and you receive an instant Telegram notification.
4 Making a Sale

Go to Sales → New Sale to record a sale.

  1. Search for the product
  2. Set the quantity and confirm the selling price
  3. Add a note if needed (e.g. customer name or job reference)
  4. Click Complete Sale

Stock is reduced automatically and the sale is saved with its revenue and profit figures.

Viewing past sales

Go to Sales to browse all recorded sales with date, items, seller, and totals.

5 Staff Accounts

Create separate accounts for your staff under Settings → Users.

RoleWhat they can do
SellerMake sales and check stock only. Cannot see revenue, profit, or financial data.
OwnerFull access — dashboard, financials, settings, analytics, Telegram bot.

Adding a staff member

  1. Go to Settings → Users
  2. Click Add User
  3. Enter their name, a username, and a password
  4. Set role to Seller
  5. Click Save — they can log in immediately
Each staff member uses their own username and password to log in to both the app and the Telegram bot. Keep their credentials private.
6 Telegram Bot

Your Telegram bot has already been set up and connected to your account by your support contact. You just need to log in and start using it.

Logging in to the bot

  1. Open Telegram on your phone
  2. Search for your bot name (shown on the cover page of this guide)
  3. Send /start
  4. It will ask for your password — send your HM Stocks login password
  5. You are signed in for 24 hours. After that, send your password again to continue.
Each person logs in with their own password. Sellers can only check stock. Owner sees financial summaries, prices, and cost details.

7 Changing Your Password

Any user can change their own password at any time.

  1. Go to Settings → Password
  2. Enter your current password
  3. Enter your new password and confirm it
  4. Click Change Password
After changing your password, you will need to use the new password next time you log in to the Telegram bot.
